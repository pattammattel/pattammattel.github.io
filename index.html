bio

I am a research professional with experiece in the areas of materials chemistry, spectroscopy and imaging.Check out my Recent research.

I was born and grown up in a village near Cochin, Kerala, India. After my masters degree in Chemistry, I joined University of Connecticut for PhD. Currently I work at the National Synchrotron Light Source - II at the Brookhaven National Laboratory, New York. My recent research is on synchrotron X-ray spectromicroscopy and machine learning assisted data analaysis.

I started doing scientific programming for last few years and a big fan of making machines work for us!

research

At the NSLS-II's Hard X-ray Nanoprobe, I led the development of spatially resolved hard X-ray nano-XANES for chemical speciation of nanoscale materials
